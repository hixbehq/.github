# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, making pull requests, updating documentation, or helping others in the community.

We adopt the Contributor Covenant v2.1 to define expected behavior.

## Our standards

- Be friendly and patient.
- Be welcoming: we strive to be inclusive of people with diverse backgrounds and identities.
- Be respectful: no harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior.
- Assume good faith: treat others with the benefit of the doubt.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following are examples of unacceptable behavior by participants:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Violence, threats of violence, or violent language.
- Posting sexually explicit or violent material.
- Posting personally identifying information (doxing).
- Sustained disruption of discussion.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at `hello@hixbe.example` (replace with the appropriate contact). All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will remain confidential to the extent possible.

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include temporary or permanent ban from the community, removal of privileges, or other actions as deemed appropriate by the maintainers.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.
